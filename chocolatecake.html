Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 & 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 & 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot coffee or hot water
  • 2 teaspoons espresso powder (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease two 9 inch pans
  3. Cream together until smooth
  4. Whisk together flour, cocoa powder and sugar
  5. Add baking soda and baking power
  6. Add salt and espresso powder
  7. Mix well, then set aside
  8. Grab a hand held or stand mixer
  9. Mix eggs, oil, and vanilla on medium-high until smooth
  10. Add buttermilk, mix until combined
  11. Pour wet ingredients into dry
  12. Add your hot water/coffee
  13. Mix until smooth and divide into pans
  14. Bake for about 25 minutes
