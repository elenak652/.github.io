Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix butter, white sugar, and brown sugar
  3. Cream together until smooth
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time
  5. Stir in the vanilla
  6. Dissolve baking soda in hot water
  7. Add to batter along with salt
  8. Stir in flour and chocolate chips
  9. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans
  10. Bake for about 10 minutes
