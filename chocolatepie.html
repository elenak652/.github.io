Chocolate Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 24 oreos
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 & 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups whole milk
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 6 & 1/2 ounces bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 can whipped cream

Steps:

  1. Start by making the pie crust
  2. Crush oreos finely
  3. Melt 4 of your 6 tablespoons of butter
  4. Add those 4 tablespoons to your oreos
  5. Put this mix into your pie pan
  6. Push down and around the pan until crust is formed
  7. Now start on your filling
  8. Whisk sugar, cornstarch and salt in saucepan
  9. Pour in milk and egg yolks
  10. Stir over medium heat until thick, 6-8 minutes
  11. Remove from heat
  12. Chop your chocolate into small peices
  13. Add chocolate, vanilla and leftover 2 tbsp butter
  14. Stir until combined
  15. Pour into pie crust
  16. Chill for 4 hours
  17. Serve with whipped cream
